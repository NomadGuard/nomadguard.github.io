Protection Against Network Attacks

DNS Spoofing/Cache Poisoning

Our Private DNS services protect against DNS spoofing attacks by validating DNS (DNSSEC) responses and ensuring they come from legitimate sources.

Man-in-the-Middle (MitM) Attacks

Encrypted DNS (DoH) mitigates the risk of MitM attacks by securing DNS queries and responses, making it impossible for attackers to intercept and manipulate traffic.

Pharming Attacks

By filtering out malicious websites, our private DNS services prevents pharming attacks that redirect your to fake websites designed to steal sensitive information.

DNS Tunneling

Some DNS-based network attacks involve tunneling malicious traffic through DNS. Our Private DNS services detect and block such attempts.

Data Exfiltration

Encrypted DNS adds a layer of protection against DNS-based data exfiltration techniques that cybercriminals might use to send stolen data to command and control servers.

Botnet Command and Control

Filtering out known malicious domains helps prevent infected devices from communicating with botnet command and control servers via DNS.
